OUR MISSION

Camp Highland crafts impactful experiences that develop leaders and engage the heart through summer camps, family retreats, group retreats and corporate events.

OUR VISION

What does it mean to engage the heart? To awaken a passion within each person, family, and group that comes to Camp Highland, leaving them focused on pursuing an authentic walk with Jesus Christ.






Our History

In 1993, while Bill was in software sales, he began taking kids on camping trips to his dad’s property. He quickly began to see the lives of kids changed and impacted in incredible ways through outdoor adventure. Through those early trips, Bill and Betsy caught a vision that would change their lives forever; thus Camp Highland was born! Now, Bill’s vision and dedication of investing full-time in building the Kingdom through outdoor ministry is continuously molding camp into a vibrant place to grow.
